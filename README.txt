== Description ==

The Phantom Writer Plugin is a powerful tool designed to automate the process of generating posts and custom post types in WordPress. Leveraging the cutting-edge GPT-4 AI model, this plugin revolutionizes content creation by providing an intelligent and efficient solution.

With Phantom Writer, you can effortlessly increase your productivity by generating engaging titles and completing posts automatically. The plugin seamlessly integrates the GPT-3.5 and GPT-4 models with WordPress, ensuring that the generated content is coherent, follows SEO best practices, and meets your language preferences.

The plugin offers an intuitive interface where you can customize various parameters, such as post length, keyword density, and tone. It empowers you to focus on high-level tasks while the AI takes care of the time-consuming process of content creation.

= KEY FEATURES =
- Automated post-generation based on the powerful GPT-4 AI model
- Generate SEO-friendly titles and posts in your preferred language
- Fine-tune parameters for post length, keyword density, and tone
- Increase productivity and save valuable time

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Q: How does the Phantom Writer Plugin work? =
A: The plugin utilizes the advanced GPT-4 AI model to automatically generate posts and custom post types. It analyzes your input, applies natural language processing, and delivers coherent and SEO-friendly content.

= Q: Can I customize the generated content? =
A: Absolutely! The Phantom Writer Plugin offers a range of customization options. You can fine-tune parameters such as post length, keyword density, and tone to align with your preferences and requirements.

= Q: Is the generated content optimized for SEO? =
A: Yes, the plugin generates content that follows SEO best practices. It ensures that your posts contain relevant keywords, proper headings, meta tags, and other essential elements to enhance your search engine visibility.

= Q: How can I view the generated results and metrics? =
A: You can find the generated results through the "Phantom Results" menu in the plugin. It allows you to review and utilize the content generated by the prompt. Additionally, both the "Phantom Results" and "Phantom Writer" menus provide metrics such as the total number of generated words and the history of previously generated texts.

= Q: Are there any additional resources available for the plugin? =
A: We are actively working on providing comprehensive documentation and additional resources to assist users in making the most of the Phantom Writer Plugin. Stay tuned for updates on our website.

= Q: What are the benefits of the premium version? =
A: The premium version offers numerous benefits, such as an increased word limit and integration with SEO plugins like Yoast and Rank Math.

= Using the Plugin =

Once activated, you can utilize the Phantom Writer Plugin to generate content effortlessly. Follow these steps:

1. Go to any post or custom post type in your WordPress admin.
2. Use our intuitive prompt interface to enter your desired content parameters.
3. Let the plugin generate the title and content based on your input.

= Viewing Generated Results and Metrics =

In addition to content generation, the Phantom Writer Plugin provides access to the generated results and metrics. You can find the generated results through the "Phantom Results" menu, where you can view and utilize the content generated by the prompt. Furthermore, both the "Phantom Results" and "Phantom Writer" menus offer metrics, such as the total number of generated words or the history of previously generated texts.
